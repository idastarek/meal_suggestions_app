Lentil bolognese

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 250g brown lentils
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 can canned tomatos
  • basil
  • salt and pepper

Substitutes

  • 250g green lentils
  • any onion
  • fresh tomatos, passata
  • basil
  • salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Dice the onion and fry on a touch of oil
